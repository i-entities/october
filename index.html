my girl

I fell further and further.

Every day with you is a gift

that I would trade nothing else for.

To say I love you

is not enough.

There are not enough words

in the english dictionary;

but I can tell you a few of them:

  1. soul: the spiritual or immaterial part of a human being or animal, regarded as immortal.
  2. spirit: those qualities regarded as forming the definitive or typical elements in the character of a person, nation
  3. life: vitality, vigor, or energy.

You are my soul. You are my spirit. You are my life.


You are my everything.

beautiful

You are the most beautiful girl.

There used to be an ache in my soul.

One would that swell, and break.

I would have normal mornings, and ordinary nights.

You however, made them into gold.

I have come to appreciate

all of the stars that now burn bright

and all of the colors that have returned.

In you, the world is now new,

my childhood heart, once knew.

Without you, the days become still,

and turn into aches nothing can fill.

Your voice, soft and sweet

it is what my soul repeats.

Sometimes I don't know how to show

properly what I feel, however this love,

my love for you, it bends my will.

You are not just someone dear,

you are everything that keeps me here.

My heart is yours, as it always will be.

In your love, I have found all of me.
